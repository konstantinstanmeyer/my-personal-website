John Martin Reservoir:

Also known as the "Sapphire on the Plains", John Martin

Reservoir is known for birdwatching, being home to over

four hundred species. There are also 213 campgrounds nearby,

giving almost endless opportunity to enjoy a night on the water.

This lake has a volume of 539 thousand acre-feet.
