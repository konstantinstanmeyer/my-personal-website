Navajo Reservoir

Located on the New Mexico border, the Navajo Reservoir

contains ~1.7 million acre-feet of water, and spans across

an area of 15,600 acres. It is situated almost perfectly

halfway across both border; This water is fishable, has

cliff diving and plenty of lodging nearby.
